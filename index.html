About Me

Hello! My name is Godfrey Hogan Eyo, and I am from Nigeria. I love eating, watching movies, and playing games in my free time. My favorite sport is table tennis, which I enjoy both for fun and as a way to stay active. I am currently majoring in Software Development, and my dream is to become a skilled Fullstack Developer. I enjoy solving problems with code, building creative projects, and learning new technologies that can make life easier and more exciting. Beyond coding, I have a strong interest in technology, innovation, and personal growth. I also value teamwork, creativity, and continuous learning, as I believe they are key to success in the tech world. One day, I hope to use my skills not just to build amazing software, but also to inspire and mentor others who are passionate about technology.
