Frey Kicks - Website Plan

Site Name

Frey Kicks

Reason for Selection: The name Frey Kicks conveys exclusivity, confidence, and a strong fashion identity. It suggests a modern sneaker brand that blends streetwear culture with luxury design.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Frey Kicks website is to promote and sell premium sneakers designed for style-conscious individuals. The site will include a product gallery, style blog, contact form, and brand story. It aims to provide an elegant shopping experience that showcases craftsmanship and innovation in footwear design.

Scenarios

  • Where can I browse the newest luxury sneaker collections from Frey Kicks?
  • How can I check available sizes or track my order after purchase?
  • What materials and design inspirations are used in Frey Kicks products?

Color Scheme

The Frey Kicks color scheme combines luxury and modern minimalism.

  • Gold (#D4AF37) - Used for highlights, buttons, borders, and key design accents.
  • Black (#0D0D0D) – Used for headings, navigation bar, and footer background.
  • Off-white (#FDFBF7) - Used for the site background to maintain a soft, premium aesthetic.

Typography

The website will use elegant and modern fonts to reflect the brand’s luxury identity.

  • Playfair Display (serif): Used for headings and titles to create a premium, editorial look.
  • Montserrat (sans-serif): Used for body text, product descriptions, and navigation for clarity and balance.
