Site Plan: Abuja Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Abuja Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a central hub for businesses in Abuja, Nigeria. It will provide resources for entrepreneurs, promote local events, highlight investment opportunities, host business directories, and encourage collaboration among enterprises.

Target Market

Business owners, corporate organizations, investors, professionals, and new residents in Abuja and surrounding areas.

Site Goals

  • Improve engagement with chamber members through resources and event promotion.
  • Attract investors to Abuja’s growing economy.
  • Enhance visibility of Abuja as a business hub.
  • Provide a trusted platform for networking and collaboration.

User Personas

  • Aisha (32): Runs a café. Needs marketing support and networking.
  • Emeka (50): Multinational executive. Seeks investment and partnerships.
  • Chinedu (27): New resident. Looking for services, events, and community opportunities.
  • Fatima (42): International investor. Needs credible business data.

Scenarios

  • A new entrepreneur wants to register as a member and checks for benefits and fees.
  • An investor browses for information on business opportunities and economic incentives.
  • A corporate executive registers for an upcoming networking event.
  • A new resident uses the directory to find local services and businesses.

SEO Plan

  • Use keywords like “Abuja business opportunities” and “Invest in Abuja.”
  • Optimize meta descriptions and alt text for local visibility.
  • Get backlinks from Abuja-based businesses and media outlets.
  • Embed Google Analytics and verify site with Google Business Profile.

Design Brief

Primary Color: #004080 (Deep Abuja Blue)

Secondary Color: #FFD700 (Nigerian Gold)

Background Color: #F5F5F5 (Light Gray)

Text Color: #000000 (Black)

Font Family: Open Sans, sans-serif and Raleway, sans-serif
